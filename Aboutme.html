Hi, I'm Karan Kr.

Hi, I'm Karan Kumar — a WordPress & Shopify Developer who enjoys building websites that are not just visually good but also fast, scalable, and actually useful for businesses.

 

My journey started with curiosity around how websites work and gradually turned into building real products, solving client problems, and working on projects used by actual users. Over time, I moved from learning HTML and CSS to developing complete websites, custom features, Shopify stores, WordPress plugins, and handling server deployments.

 

Today, I have delivered 50+ websites across WordPress and Shopify and gained nearly 3 years of hands-on experience working on client projects, eCommerce stores, enterprise websites, custom dashboards, and plugin development.

 

I currently work with technologies like WordPress, Shopify, PHP, JavaScript, MySQL, Liquid, WooCommerce, and custom development workflows, while continuously exploring better ways to build faster, scalable, and performance-focused web experiences.

 

What I enjoy most is turning ideas into working products — whether it's redeveloping existing websites for better performance, creating custom functionalities, or improving user experience to deliver better results.

 

Outside of development, I spend time exploring new technologies, experimenting with projects, and improving the way I build things. I'm always excited about opportunities where I can create, solve problems, and continue growing as a developer.
